The question I’m trying to tackle: Is it wise for a 20-something year old to take on a big mortgage in a foreign country?

Relevant pieces of information about me.

  • I’m a software developer who can work remotely
  • I live in Europe, though my home country is Australia

What have I learnt in this process?

  1. Having time constraints on attempting to buy property is extremely unwise. At some point in this process, I found myself willing the problem away by trying to buy quickly without thoughtful consideration about the parameters of the apartment. Taking out several hundred thousand euros in debt without being crystal-clear in one’s motivations and future plans is surely a one-way ticket to regret.
